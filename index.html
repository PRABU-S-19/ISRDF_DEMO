About ISRDF

The International Social Research & Development Foundation (ISRDF) is a registered non-profit trust committed to creating meaningful, lasting change in society.

Our Vision

"To create a just, inclusive, and sustainable society where education, health, environment, and economic empowerment together enable every individual to lead a dignified and self-reliant life."

Our Mission

"To conduct meaningful research, design practical interventions, and deliver community-based programs across education, health, economy, agriculture, environment, criminology, and public utilities."

Our Approach

We believe in one simple principle: real change begins when knowledge meets compassion. We work at the grassroots, research before acting, and empower over assist.
